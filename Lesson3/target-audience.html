Design Index Audience

Target Audience:

The target audience for the Mountain View Chamber of Commerce website would be individuals, businesses, and organizations
that are interested in the economic and social development of Mountain View, Utah. This may include local entrepreneurs, small business owners,
non-profit organizations, government agencies, residents, and visitors to the area. The website would serve as a hub for information and resources
on the local economy, events, and community initiatives. It would also provide networking opportunities, business support services,
and promote the city's unique characteristics and attractions. Ultimately, the goal of the website would be to promote and enhance the economic vitality
and quality of life in Mountain View, Utah.

Scenarios:

1. Business owners and entrepreneurs looking for resources: One scenario where someone might want to use the Mountain View
Chamber of Commerce website is if they are a business owner or entrepreneur looking for resources to help them start, grow,
or sustain their business. The website could provide information on local business regulations, access to funding,
networking opportunities, and training programs.

2. Local residents looking for events and activities: Another scenario is if someone is a local resident looking for events
and activities in the area. The website could provide a calendar of upcoming events, information on local attractions, and
directories of restaurants and shops to explore.

3. Tourists planning a visit: Tourists planning a visit to Mountain View, Utah may also want to use the
Chamber of Commerce website to learn more about the area. They could find information on lodging options, local tours, and recommendations for
restaurants and activities to experience.

4. Non-profit organizations seeking support: Non-profit organizations may also want to use the website to seek
support from the local business community. The website could provide information on local sponsorship opportunities, volunteer opportunities,
and ways to partner with businesses for mutual benefit.

Overall, the Mountain View Chamber of Commerce website can be a valuable resource for anyone looking to connect with
the local community, whether they are starting a business, looking for something to do, or seeking ways to support the city's economic
and social development.
